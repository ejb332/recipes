The “Original” Green River® Beer Margarita
Ingredients:
1 (12 oz) can frozen limeade concentrate (semi-thawed)
6 oz Premium Tequila
2 -12oz bottles of Green River® Soda
12 fluid ounces light beer.
Ice (lots of ice!)
1 lime (cut into wedges)
Preparation:
Pour the limeade, tequila, Green River® Soda, and beer into a large glass pitcher; stir with a long-handled spoon until completely blended and the frozen limeade has completely melted. Add in lots of ice. Adjust with extra light beer to taste if the mixture is too sweet. Serve each glass with a lime wedge on the end.
Go team!
